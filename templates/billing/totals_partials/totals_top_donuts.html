KT margin — what we made vs still owed

These figures are KT’s share on each booking (from stored subtotal where present). Paid is all recorded payment money linked to the bookings shown in this section. Unpaid starts from bookings still marked unpaid, then subtracts payments on those same unpaid bookings (never below zero per booking). Shuttle uses trip price as margin until costs are modeled separately.

How to read these cards

Total (center of donut)

Your full KT margin for confirmed bookings in that bucket.

Paid

All payment money recorded for those bookings in that time range.

Unpaid

KT margin still open on bookings marked unpaid, minus any partial payments on those same bookings.
