Totals

KT margin — what we made vs still owed

These figures are KT’s share on each booking (from stored subtotal where present). Paid is all recorded payment money linked to the bookings shown in this section. Unpaid starts from bookings still marked unpaid, then subtracts payments on those same unpaid bookings (never below zero per booking). Shuttle uses trip price as margin until costs are modeled separately.

How to read these cards

Total (center of donut)

Your full KT margin for confirmed bookings in that bucket.

Paid

All payment money recorded for those bookings in that time range.

Unpaid

KT margin still open on bookings marked unpaid, minus any partial payments on those same bookings.

Driving / Shuttle / Hotel

Each card shows that service type only; snapshots combine all three.

Customer gross (GMV)

Separate from KT margin. Totals are what customers are charged (driving job price, shuttle price, hotel customer pays), in EUR. Paid is all recorded payment money linked to bookings in this section; unpaid is the remaining open value on bookings still marked unpaid, after subtracting payments on those same unpaid bookings.

What GMV means

Total customer charge value, not KT profit.

Paid

Recorded customer payments applied to those bookings.

Unpaid

Charge value still outstanding on unpaid bookings after their own recorded payments are deducted.

When to use this

Use GMV for sales volume, and KT margin cards for your actual business share.
